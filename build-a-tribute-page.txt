          <li>
            <strong>1954</strong> : Graduated from Saint Joseph’s College, Tiruchirappalli.
          </li>
          <li>
            <strong>1960</strong> : Joined the Aeronautical Development Establishment of the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO).
          </li>
          <li>
            <strong>1969</strong> : Transferred to the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and became the project director of India’s first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III).
          </li>
          <li>
            <strong>1982</strong> : Rejoined DRDO and led the Integrated Guided Missile Development Program (IGMDP).
          </li>
          <li>
            <strong>1992-1999</strong> : Served as the Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister and the Secretary of the DRDO.
          </li>
          <li>
            <strong>1998</strong> : Played a pivotal role in India’s nuclear tests at Pokhran, making India a nuclear power.
          </li>
          <li>
            <strong>2002-2007</strong> : Served as the President of India.
          </li>
          <li>
            <strong>2015</strong> : Passed away on July 27 in Shillong, Meghalaya, while delivering a lecture.
          </li>
        </ul>
